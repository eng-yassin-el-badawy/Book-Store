The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck

Mark Manson

The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck is a self-help book by Mark Manson that challenges conventional wisdom about happiness and success. The book argues that people should focus on what truly matters to them and let go of societal expectations and pressures. Manson emphasizes the importance of embracing life's struggles and accepting that not everything can be controlled. The book encourages readers to prioritize their values, set boundaries, and find meaning in their lives by caring less about trivial things and more about what truly brings them fulfillment.
